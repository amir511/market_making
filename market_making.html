Tune the strategy

Turn skew off and watch inventory wander to the cap — risk balloons even though the spread still pays. Turn it on to see quotes lean against the position. Fire a toxic flow burst to feel adverse selection bite.
What this is. A simplified simulation of a continuous two-sided market maker, in the spirit of the Avellaneda–Stoikov model. Ordinary fills arrive from uninformed flow whose intensity falls as a quote sits further from fair value, so the spread is genuinely profitable on a random walk. Real desks add layers (order-book depth, latency, queue position, multiple venues, correlated hedges) this leaves out. Numbers are illustrative, not trading advice.
